About This Project

This dashboard showcases some information about the bacteria found in participants navels. This information will be used to identify potential strains of bacteria that synthesize proteins that mimic the flavor of some meats. The graphs visualize the 10 most populous bacteria cultures in the samples as well as the total number of each culture by the relative size of bubbles. Also pictured is the frequency at which volunteers washed their belly buttons.

The charts provided are plotted by their Operational Taxonomic Unit (OTU) identifaction numbers. For more specific data, please hover for the full OTU label.
